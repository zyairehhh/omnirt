# ADR 0001: v0.1 Scope

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

`omnirt` v0.1 focuses on the minimum image and video runtime abstraction with CUDA and Ascend backends. Service features, hot-swapping adapters, and graph-level optimization are intentionally deferred.

## Consequences

- the codebase optimizes for clear component and backend boundaries first
- heavy model wiring can land incrementally without reworking the public request/result contract
- tests are split into unit, integration, and parity tiers from day one
